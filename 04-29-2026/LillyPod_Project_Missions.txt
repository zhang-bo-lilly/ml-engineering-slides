LillyPod — Project Missions | April 6, 2026
============================================

ai_neuro
Building a multimodal AI model for Alzheimer's disease that integrates imaging, genomic, and clinical data to improve patient screening, stratification, and disease progression prediction in Lilly trials.

alchemy
Scaling genomic language models to full training capacity to enable genome-wide impact prediction for human genetic variation.

disc-imaging
Automating the analysis of imaging assay data across the drug discovery pipeline — from in vitro phenotypic profiling to in vivo tissue mapping — to accelerate development decisions.

disc-stats
Building scalable agentic AI orchestration frameworks to accelerate scientific research workflows, including antibody design and knowledge synthesis.

embodied-ai
Evaluating NVIDIA's embodied AI ecosystem to identify how emerging robotics, vision, and simulation technologies can be applied within Lilly.

genai-comm
Developing language models for adverse event and product complaint recognition from commercial communications to support regulatory drug safety decisions.

genai-gs
Powering enterprise generative AI capabilities through two tracks: fine-tuning open-source language models for business domains, and continuous pretraining to build Lilly's own internal foundation model.

genai-litlens
Operating LitLens, Lilly's AI-powered semantic search platform across scientific literature, patents, and internal documents.

large-mol
Training protein folding and design models on all publicly available protein structures to accelerate in-silico protein design campaigns.

lgm
Developing a unified model for gene-silencing molecule potency ranking and off-target analysis, alongside property models and codon optimization for mRNA and gene therapy modalities.

remedi
Developing domain-adapted language models for pharma applications — including scientific literature intelligence (Pubs.AI) and regulatory compliance checking (ConsiCheck) — alongside agentic AI research for pharmacovigilance and regulatory workflows.

single-cell
Scaling a virtual cell foundation model to 160 million cells to enable concurrent, large-scale experimentation on cell biology AI.

small-mol
Pre-training a graph-based molecular foundation model on over one billion molecules to enable generalizable property prediction across diverse chemical space.

tunelab-ai
Training chemistry and antibody molecular models and developing a universal protein-small molecule binding model to advance computational drug design.
